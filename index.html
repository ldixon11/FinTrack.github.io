FinTrack

FinTrack is a tool directed at addressing the lack of clarity around the allocation and availability of climate funds to countries. FinTrack uses historic use of climate funds across all countries, as well as indicative allocations across both climate funds, and from development fund climate commitments to highlight resources available to address climate financing gaps. FinTrack also aims to assess the barriers to access these funds, both with regard to the concessionality of these resources, and the criteria required to access these funds. Overall, this aims to help countries identify and prioritise larger volumes of accessible climate funds, increasing the financial feasibility of sustainable transitions.
